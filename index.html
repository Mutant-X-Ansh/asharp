A SHARP MUSIC

Universal Language of Music with ‘A Sharp’

A Sharp invites you to experience the unique adventure of music. We are an extremely talented and spirited group of individuals with a vast experience in the music industry. With the help of our professional faculty, industry-inspired facilities, world-class courses the students can take their first step into a future of musical possibilities. Our team is well equipped and qualified to teach western vocals, Indian classical music, piano, guitar, and drums. We believe that the best music comes from the heart and that feelings expressed through the medium of music are the best kind.

Our Values

We aspire quality music education available to everyone, especially to those who have passion but can’t afford because of unfortunate circumstances. We strive to equip the children with all the knowledge they need to turn their musical passion into something more than a hobby. We believe that everyone is capable of learning music and reaching majestic heights with their efforts. We believe in making the music learning process fun-filled, joyful and memorable. A Sharp wants to train the next generation of musicians, artists, composers to leave their impression on India’s burgeoning music scene.

A Sharp team has set out to spread the love of music throughout India. We are offering a platform and an open opportunity for all the students who love music and even those who have no clue about music. We imagine a bright future where music is considered a way of life, passion and not just another hobby. We want to make sure our students have more than one career option to look forward to when they pass out of school. With the progressive initiative of schools throughout India as well as passionate and resourceful team of A Sharp, we want to build a future where everyone can connect through the common thread of music.

Composer of Children’s Musical Journey

1. With the rise in social media and entertainment platforms, more and more parents, as well as schools, have started to recognise the importance of children’s extracurricular talents.
2. A Sharp is partnering with multiple schools that want to include music to their curriculum for the all-round growth of their students.
3. For the program to be more immersive and engaging we have devised an evaluation system to help children choose their musical instrument.
4. Students will be asked to pick a musical instrument of their choice to play and then through our proprietary tests, we will evaluate whether that particular instrument is suitable for the student.
5. Our evaluation also helps in finding out the hidden talents within the students that have gone unnoticed before and it helps us in understanding the inclination of the students towards a particular instrument.
6. Affiliation is very easy as our team provides the following services:

a. Arranging instruments like a keyboard, piano, guitar, etc.
b. Designing the interior of the classroom to attract students and keep them engaged for a longer period.
c. Employee management of the music instructors, including their salaries and scheduling.
d. Designing the curriculum according to the needs of the school and provide only the best musical learning to students.

Music to Our Partner’s Ears (Benefits)

  • Schools affiliated with us are in a unique position to market themselves as one of the few schools that include music as a part of their curriculum.
  • The program is self-managed and once our team has set up everything on the school premises; the administration does not have to worry about the day-to-day operation of the program.
  • We design the curriculum considering the artistic aspect as well as the technical aspect of the music industry. The curriculum also benefits students for their career advancements.
  • The school administration is involved in every step of designing the curriculum and can choose what their students learn.
  • We also organise musicals and plays periodically to promote the use of music in daily life. Through participation, students gain stage confidence and better communication.
  • We teach a comprehensive blend of classical as well as modern Western music, providing a wide variety to students.
  • Our team comprises the best musical instructors with a comprehensive experience in the music industry.
  • A Sharp is committed to tailoring the music lessons as per schools’ interests via finding out their individual musical goals and curriculum.
  • We have managed an array of professional musicians in the past, sharing their enthusiasm and passion for best quality music education. Therefore, we can provide the best musical learning to the students.
  • We believe in creating a friendly environment that makes students comfortable with their surroundings helping them imbibe the lessons more easily.
  • We encourage students to interact with the instructors as well as their peers to gain knowledge more readily.
  • Our team promotes individuality and helps students play the instruments they are comfortable with.
